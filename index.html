Most Asked Questions

1. what is insurance service?

Insurance is a contract, represented by a policy, in which an individual or entity receives financial protection or reimbursement against losses from an insurance company.

2.How do I choose the right insurance policy?

Consider factors such as coverage needs, policy limits, premiums, deductibles, and the reputation of the insurance company.

3. What is a premium?

A premium is the amount you pay for your insurance policy, typically on a monthly, quarterly, or annual basis.

4. How do I file an insurance claim?

Contact your insurance company, provide details of the incident, and follow their instructions for submitting necessary documentation.

5. What is a deductible?

A deductible is the amount you pay out of pocket before your insurance policy pays for any covered losses.

6. What is a policy limit?

A policy limit is the maximum amount an insurance company will pay for a covered loss.
